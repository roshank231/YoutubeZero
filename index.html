How to Use

1. Find a Template (Right Panel):
Start on the right side. You can filter by category (All, New, Old), use the subcategory chips, or search for a template by name.

2. Generate a Search:
Click "Pick" on any template to see a preview. The main "Find Random Videos" button will use your selected template. If no template is picked, it will choose one for you based on your filters.

3. Customize (Optional):
Use the toggles below to control variable randomization and sorting. Turn off randomization to enter your own values (like a specific date) in the "Variable / Overrides" box on the right.
